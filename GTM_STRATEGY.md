## Phase 2: Lead Generation (Weeks 3-6)

### LinkedIn Outreach (Primary Channel)
**Target:** Small strata managers in NSW, VIC, QLD (under 200 lots)

**Daily Activity:**
- 20 connection requests/day to strata managers
- Personalised message template:

> "Hi [Name], I noticed you manage strata portfolios in [Region]. I've been working with strata managers to reduce their admin time by 40%+ through AI automation. Would you be open to a quick 15-min chat about your biggest workflow pain points? No pitch — just curious about your experience."

**Content Strategy:**
- Post 3x/week on LinkedIn:
  - Monday: Industry insight/statistic
  - Wednesday: Problem-solving tip
  - Friday: Case study/result

### Email Campaign
**Lead Magnet:** Free Compliance Checklist → email capture → nurture sequence

**Sequence (5 emails over 14 days):**
1. Here's your compliance checklist (day 0)
2. The hidden cost of disconnected tools (day 2)
3. How one Sydney manager saved 15hrs/week (day 5)
4. Free 30-min compliance audit invitation (day 7)
5. Last chance — audit spots for this month (day 14)

### Partnerships
**Target Partners:**
- Onsite.fm — they have workflow gaps, we fill them
- Intellistrata — similar opportunity
- Strata Community Association chapters
- Property management conferences (REI Convention, StrataLive)

**Partnership Model:**
- Referral fee: 15% of first 3 months' revenue
- Co-branded content: "The Future of Strata Tech" webinar series

## Phase 3: Conversion (Weeks 5-12)

### Free Compliance Audit → Paid Conversion
**Process:**
1. Prospect books free 30-min audit
2. We analyse their current tools, workflows, compliance gaps
3. Present findings with specific automation opportunities
4. Recommend plan (Starter/Growth/Enterprise)
5. Close on the spot or follow-up within 48 hours

**Conversion Target:** 30% of audits → paying clients

### Pricing Strategy
| Tier | Price | Target | Margin |

| Starter | $500/mo | < 100 lots | ~70% |
| Growth | $1,000/mo | 100-500 lots | ~75% |

**Setup Fees:** $2,000-$5,000 one-time (covers onboarding, integration, training)
